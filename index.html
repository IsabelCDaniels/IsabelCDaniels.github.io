About Me
Hi my name is Isabel Daniels. I am a senior at UC Berkeley studying Electical Engineering and Computer Science. Welcome to my website that I created entirly from scratch using HTML, CSS, and Javascript! I started my college journey by attending Pasadena City College in Southern California for two years before transfering to UC Berkeley. While at PCC I was able to obtain my Associtate degree in physics, mathematics, and computer science. During my time there I became the president of the Computer Science Club and sat on the board of She.Codes, a club aimed at encouraging women to learn about computer science and STEM. As a leader of these clubs I lead weekly meetings and put on large events like PCC's first ever Hackathon and our Annual #Include Women in CS confrence. While living in Los Angeles, I also interned for Equallet, a search engine for women owned businesses that was focused on achieving economic gender equality. As an intern, I worked developing verification systems for their Database and helped provide technical direction for Equallet 2.0.
